# Friendly user trial — Overview

This is a **controlled trial** of the first narrow release: **Operations reporting assistant**. You are a trusted early user helping us learn what works and what doesn’t. Nothing you do is sent to the cloud; everything stays on your machine.

---

## What the product does (right now)

- **Summarizes your reporting workflow** — Based on your projects and style signals, it suggests how you might structure recurring reporting and weekly status.
- **Suggests next steps** — From your project and routine context, it recommends sensible next actions (suggestions only; it does not run anything).
- **Explains workflow type** — If you use certain file types (e.g. .csv, .xlsx), it can describe what kind of workflow that might be (e.g. operations/data).
- **Generates scaffolds in a sandbox** — It can produce a status-report or ops-handoff structure in a local sandbox folder. Nothing is written to your real project paths unless you explicitly approve an “apply” step.

---

## What it does not do

- It does **not** create or fill spreadsheets from live data.
- It does **not** do creative briefs, storyboards, or design packages (those are planned later).
- It does **not** sync across devices or users; it’s one user, one machine.
- It does **not** run any action without your confirmation; “apply” always requires you to confirm.

---

## What we need from you

1. **Try the tasks** we suggest (see TRIAL_TASKS.md and the quickstart).
2. **Tell us what was useful** and what was confusing or broken.
3. **Record feedback** using the trial commands or the simple forms we provide — so we can improve the product based on real use.

Your feedback is stored only **locally** (on your machine). We use it to decide whether to refine the product further or to open a slightly wider private pilot.
